#### evaluation
```

App bar: 4dp
Card: 1dp to 8dp
FAB: 6dp
Button: 2dp to 8dp
```

|Standard bottom sheet/Standard side sheet|8
|Bottom navigation bar|8
|Bottom app bar|8
|Menus and sub menus|8

|Contained button (pressed state)|8
|Floating action button (FAB - resting elevation)/Snackbar|6

|Switch|1
|Text button|0
|Standard side sheet|0
